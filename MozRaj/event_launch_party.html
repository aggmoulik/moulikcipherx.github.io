Overview

Drive your career to the forefront by learning the latest tools, techniques and technologies in front-end web development.

If you work in front-end web development, then you know how quickly the space changes. By attending Web Unleashed, you will get up-to-date on the skills needed to push you ahead in your career, and elevate your personal and professional development. You’ll also have the opportunity to network and share ideas with like-minded developers, creative coders, evangelists, designers, technologists, managers, makers, engineers, educators, directors, strategists, enthusiasts and more!

Join us at Web Unleashed for

• Two full days with up to four tracks to choose from each day
• Over 50 presentations, panels, live coding sessions, and round table discussions.
• Opportunities to network and connect, including evening events each day.

Who Should Attend

This conference is for developers who are interested in all things front-end. From technical to inspirational sessions, there’s always something for you, no matter which stage of your career you’re in.

Why Should You Attend?

Web Unleashed covers a variety of front-end topics leaving you informed, challenged and inspired. It’s two days packed with presentations, featuring hand-picked speakers from all over the world. Web Unleashed also provides unparalleled networking opportunities and parties. We bring the community together to connect, putting you in a room with people who share your passion for this industry and giving the future a stage.
